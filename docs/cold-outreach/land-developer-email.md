# Cold outreach — Day 9

Three cold emails, copy-paste, send Monday morning.

The pattern is the same for all three: 4 sentences max, "I'm building this, can I show you?", 15 minutes, no selling. Each variant personalizes the vertical + the operator persona.

**Subject:** 15 minutes on land site selection in South Africa?

15 minutes this week?

Chris

## How to use

1. Pick **3 real people** you actually know in the persona. Don't blast strangers — these are relationship-warm emails.
2. Replace `{first name}` and `{city}` with their actuals.
3. Send Monday morning, 9-10am local time.
4. If no reply in 4 days, follow up once: "Bumping this — saw the message below. Still interested in 15 minutes?" Then stop.
5. Whoever replies with even a soft "maybe", get them on a 15-min call. Demo Atlas live. Ask: "What would make this worth R250/mo for you?"

## What success looks like

- 0 replies → you sent to the wrong people. Adjust vertical or ask a friend for an introduction.
- 1 reply + 1 call → that's 1 design partner. Get them to use Atlas for free for 1 month in exchange for weekly feedback. **This is your first customer.**
- 1 reply that becomes a paying customer → $250-2,500/mo MRR. **This is your traction.**

## After 5 design partners

You'll have enough signal to:
1. Refine the product based on real use
2. Validate pricing
3. Have 5 customer testimonials for the investor pitch
4. Decide which vertical to deepen (gas station, restaurant, warehouse, retail?)

That's when you raise.
